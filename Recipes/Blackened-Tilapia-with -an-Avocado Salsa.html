Blackened Tilapia with an Avocado Salsa

Ingredients (4 servings):

  • 2 tbsp. packed brown sugar
  • 4 tsp. ground cumin
  • 2 tsp. kosher salt
  • 2 tsp. Paprika
  • 2 tsp. dried oregano
  • 1 tsp. freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp. garlic powder
  • 4 (6-oz.) tilapia filets
  • 2 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 avocados (diced)
  • 2 tomatoes (diced)
  • 1 jalape√±o (minced)
  • 2 tbsp. chopped cilantro
  • 2 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Kosher salt

Directions:

  1. Mix brown sugar, cumin, paprika, garlic powder, black pepper, and oregano in a small bowl.
  2. Coat filets in the mixture.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet before adding the coated filets.
  4. Cook until filets are golden and flake easily (2-3 minutes per side).
  5. For the salsa, combine ingredients in a medium sized bowl.
  6. Make sure to thoroughly mix ingredients.
  7. Serve filets on a plate before topping with salsa.

Health Benefits:

Tilapia contains omega-3 fatty acids which are healthy fats that your body needs to survive. It is full of choline, vitamin B12 and D, selenium, and phosphorus. Selenium plays a role in cancer prevention, while choline helps maintain nerve and brain health.
