Sheet Pan Omelet

Ingredients (6 servings):

  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • ½ cup milk
  • 12 eggs
  • ½ cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups bell pepper
  • ½ cup cubed meat (bacon, sausage, ham, etc)

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350, pull out baking sheet and spray with cooking spray.
  2. Whisk eggs, milk, salt and pepper together in medium sized bowl.
  3. Pour mixture into a pan before spreading toppings evenly throughout mixture.
  4. Bake for 20 mins or until eggs settle.
  5. Scoop out desired amount to plate to serve.

Health Benefits:

Omelets are full of protein along with being rich in choline, a compound that helps maintain nerve and brain health. They also contain Vitamins A, B, C, D, E, and K.
