Spinach and Feta Grilled Cheese

Ingredients:

  • ½ tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • ¼ lb frozen chopped spinach
  • 1 pinch of salt and pepper
  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1 oz feta

Directions:

  1. Add olive oil and minced garlic to a skillet pan and saute for one minute.
  2. Add frozen spinach and a pinch of salt and pepper and saute on medium heat until most moisture evaporates from the skillet.
  3. Spread butter on one side of the bread and put the buttered side towards heat.
  4. Divide half of the shredded mozzarella between the two sandwiches and put crumbled feta on top.
  5. Divide the cooked spinach on top of the slices of bread.
  6. Add bread on top to complete the sandwich.
  7. Heat on medium-low heat and cook unitl golden brown on the outside and gooey cheese on the inside.
  8. Cut the sandwich in half and enjoy!

Health Benefits:

This quick-to-make grilled cheese is filled with healthy ingredients with the spinach and the cheese. The mozzarella and feta cheese provide protein and other essential vitamins, specifically the feta is high in calcium which helps maintain the strength of teeth and bones. The spinach contributes antioxidants, and the bread is a carbohydrate which supplies energy. This is a well balanced meal that could be a great option for lunch or dinner.
