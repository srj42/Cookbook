Spinach Salad with Pan Seared Chicken Breast

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 skinless boneless chicken breasts
  • Salt
  • Garlic powder
  • Black pepper
  • 1 (10 ounce) pack of baby spinach leaves (rinsed and drained)
  • 1 pint Cherry tomatoes
  • 1 avocado (peeled pitted and sliced)
  • 1 yellow bell pepper (sliced)
  • 2 green onions (sliced)
  • Grated parmesan
  • Balsamic dressing

Directions

  1. Warm oil in a skillet at medium to high heat.
  2. Season chicken with garlic powder, salt and pepper.
  3. When oil is warm, place chicken firmly in the skillet.
  4. Cook for 3 to 5 minutes on each side or until the center is no longer pink.
  5. Remove chicken and let it rest for 5 minutes then slice thinly.

Health Benefits:

Spinach is rich in vitamins and antioxidants. Antioxidants and vitamins work together to strengthen the immune system. Chicken is a great source of protein. Protein provides energy and reduces appetite which prevents overeating.
