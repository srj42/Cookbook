Buffalo Chicken Sandwich

Ingredients (6 servings):

  • 2 lbs shredded chicken
  • ½ cup buffalo sauce
  • ½ cup greek yogurt
  • 2-3 tbsp honey
  • 6 sandwich buns

Directions:

  1. Combine buffalo sauce and honey in a saucepan and stir together until boil.
  2. Add chicken to the sauce and stir for 5 minutes.
  3. Remove mixture from heat before stirring in greek yogurt.
  4. Open sandwich buns and serve

Health Benefits:

Chicken is a good alternative to the red meat usually found in burgers as it has lower saturated fat. It also is high in protein along with being high in B6, B12, iron, and zinc. Greek yogurt is also rich in protein, B12, selenium, and zinc.
