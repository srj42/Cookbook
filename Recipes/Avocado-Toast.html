Avocado Toast

Ingredients (4 servings):

  • ½ cup of mashed avocado
  • ½ cup of Ricotta cheese
  • ½ teaspoon of Garlic Powder
  • ¼ teaspoon of Salt
  • ¼ teaspoon of black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon of Lemon Juice
  • 4 slices bread of choice (italian bread recommended), toasted
  • 4 poached eggs

Directions:

  1. In a medium sized bowl, combine the mashed avocado, ricotta cheese, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Fill a pot with 3 to 5 inches of water and bring to a boil.
  3. Crack one egg at a time into a small bowl.
  4. Once the water reaches a boil, take a spoon and begin to swirl the water.
  5. While water is swirling gently slide one egg into the water.
  6. Cover for 3 to 4 minutes then remove egg.
  7. Repeat this process for all eggs.
  8. Spread the avocado and ricotta mixture over toasted bread and place poached egg on top.

Health Benefits:

Avocado is a great source of fiber which is important for gut health. Additionally, avocado is high in unsaturated fats which boost cardiovascular health by lowering cholesterol.
