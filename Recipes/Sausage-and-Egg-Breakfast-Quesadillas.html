Sausage and egg Breakfast Quesadillas

Ingredients (6 servings):

  • 8 oz country sausage
  • 6 large eggs
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ pepper
  • 6 oz cheddar
  • 6 medium flour tortillas

Directions:

  1. Add the sausage to a skillet and cook on medium heat until browned and crispy on the edges.
  2. Once cooked, remove sausage and leave a little grease.
  3. In a bowl, crack six eggs and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Whisk together and shred the cheddar cheese.
  5. Put whisked eggs in the same skillet, cook the eggs over medium heat, gently folding them as the eggs set.
  6. Cook until eggs are set but moist then remove them from heat.
  7. Add the cooked sausage and shredded cheddar to the skillet, then fold the ingredients together until fully combined.
  8. Place about ½ cup egg/sauage mixture into each tortilla.
  9. Spread it out and fold the tortilla closed.
  10. Add folded tortillas back to skillet and cook over medium heat on each side until brown and crispy.
  11. Slice the quesadilla and enjoy!

Health Benefits:

This recipe is perfect for any time in the day: breakfast, lunch or dinner. These quesadillas are fast to make but provide great benefits. Eggs and sausage are easy sources for protein, and are very affordable. Need a quick meal that will fill you up, this is a perfect option.
