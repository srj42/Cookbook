Mexican Quinoa Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa uncooked
  • 2 cups water
  • 15 oz. can of black beans
  • 15 oz. can of sweet corn
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • Â¼ large red onion (minced)
  • 2 large avocados (sliced)

Directions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, bring 1 cup of quinoa and 2 cups of water to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat to low, cover, and let simmer for 20 minutes or until water is completely absorbed.
  3. Once quinoa is cooked, place in a large tupperware and let chill in the refrigerator for 2 hours.
  4. Prepare salad by mixing together cooked quinoa, black beans, sweet corn, cherry tomatoes, and red onion.
  5. Mix until combined, Then, pour on dressing of your choice and mix again.

Health Benefits:

Quinoa salad is a healthy meal idea because quinoa is high-protein and naturally gluten-free and vegan. This recipe is both vegan and vegetarian.
