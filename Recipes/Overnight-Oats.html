Overnight Oats

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • Â½ cup nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup milk of choice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1-2 tablespoons of sweetener of choice honey or maple syrup

Directions:

  1. Place all ingredients into a large glass container and mix until combined.
  2. Cover and place into the refrigerator for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  3. Eat with your favorite toppings including nut butter and berries.

Health Benefits:

Overnight oats are fiber-packed and low in sugar, making it the perfect healthy breakfast choice. Oatmeal is a whole grain and a nutritious way to start your morning. This recipe can be quickly prepped the night before, making it a convenient choice for busy college students.
