Baked Salmon

Ingredients (4 servings):

  • 1 salmon filet (2 pounds)
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened
  • ¼ cup white wine or chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • ½ teaspoon pepper
  • ½ teaspoon dried tarragon

Directions:

  1. Pat salmon dry.
  2. Place in a greased baking dish.
  3. Brush with butter.
  4. Combine the remaining ingredients and pour over salmon
  5. Bake, uncovered, at 425° for 15-20 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork.

Health Benefits:

Salmon is an excellent source of antioxidants and vitamins. It is rich in vitamin B12, which is necessary for producing red blood cells and regulating the health of the central nervous system. However, it's the fatty acids in salmon that really make it a healthy option. Omega-3 fatty acids are a type of heart-healthy fat that can decrease inflammation and support brain health.
