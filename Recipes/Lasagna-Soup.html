Lasagna Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 small yellow onion
  • 1 small diced zucchini
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Â½ tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 28 oz jar of marinara sauce
  • 15 oz can diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 2 cups water
  • 9oz of no boil lasagna noodles

Directions:

  1. In a large bowl or sauce pan, heat oil.
  2. Add onion, pepper, zucchini and stir.
  3. Cook until softened.
  4. Add garlic and ground turkey to the pan.
  5. Cook until no pink. If meat releases liquid, drain once cooked through.
  6. Add in spices and stir.
  7. Add marinara sauce, tomatoes, stock and water to the pan. Bring to a boil.
  8. At boiling point, crush lasagna pieces into smaller pisces and add to soup.
  9. Cook for 15 minutes or until noodles are fully cooked, stirring occasionally.
  10. Top with fresh parmesan or mozzarella if preferred. Refrigerate for up to 6 days.

Health Benefits:

This quick soup has all the essentials in a meal. There is lean meat with the ground turkey and a mix of vegetables. This soup provides an individual with several meals making it time efficient and healthy.
